# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

New process engineers and technical readers learning how a simplified TSV
traveler is simulated, measured, and improved.

## Product Purpose

Teach the main TSV process handoffs through saved simulation evidence. Success
means a first-time reader can connect model controls to profile changes,
measurements, failure modes, and the limits of the evidence.

## Brand Personality

Clear, technical, and candid.

## Anti-references

Dense research logs, unexplained simulator jargon, oversized prose, decorative
cartoons presented as evidence, and generic AI-generated dashboard layouts.

## Design Principles

- Show the saved simulation before explaining it.
- Prefer physical language, then give the model term when needed.
- Keep claims proportional to the saved evidence.
- Make controls and their geometric consequences visible together.
- Put provenance close to the result without interrupting the lesson.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, adequate contrast,
reduced-motion support, and labels that do not depend on color alone.
